Simple Calculator

This is a simple calculator that can perform basic math operations.

Features:
Addition (+)
Subtraction (-)
Multiplication (*)
Division (/)
Percentage calculation (%)

How to use:
Enter the first number.
Select the desired operation.
Enter the second number.
Click the equals button (=) to see the result.

Examples:
To add two numbers: 10 + 5 = 15
To subtract two numbers: 20 - 10 = 10
To multiply two numbers: 3 × 4 = 12
To divide two numbers: 15 ÷ 3 = 5
To calculate a percentage(of 100): 20% = 0.2

Additional notes:
The calculator can handle numbers of any length, including decimals.
The calculator can also handle negative numbers.
